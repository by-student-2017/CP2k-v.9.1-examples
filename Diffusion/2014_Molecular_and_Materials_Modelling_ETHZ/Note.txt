#---------------------------------------------------------------------
MSD = <[x(t)-x(0)]^2> = 2*n-dimension*D*t

For 3-dimensional Brownian motion,
i.e., n-dimension=3, and D = MSD/(6*t)

In this case, units are 
MSD [Angstrom^2]
t [fs]
D [Angstrom^2/fs] = 0.1 [cm^2/s] = 1.0e-5 [m^2/s]
