# Usage
# QM/MM with Built-in Force Field
# https://manual.cp2k.org/cp2k-2024_1-branch/methods/qm_mm/builtin.html
1. conda activate AmberTools23
2. bash run_ambertools.sh
3. bash run_cp2k.sh
4. (Open "EM-pos-1.xyz", "NVT-pos-1.xyz", etc on VMD or Ovito)
5. (Open "sqm.pdb", "Protein.pdb" or mol2 file, etc on UCSF Chimera)
